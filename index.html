About Us

A "sweet shop" is a retail establishment that primarily sells sweets and confectionery, such as candy, chocolates, and other sweet treats. It can also sometimes include other items like cigarettes, newspapers, or magazines, depending on the location and culture. In the United Kingdom, this type of store is commonly referred to as a sweet shop, while in North America, it's more often called a candy shop or candy store. In Australia and New Zealand, the term "lolly shop" is often used.
